About me

I am currently in my 5th semester in ISCL (International Studies of Computational Linguistics) at the University of Tubingen.
After completing my Bachelor’s degree, I plan to commence my Master’s in ISCL the following winter of 2025.
Throughout my academic journey, I have gained extensive experience in implementing (Deep) Neural Networks with frameworks like pytorch, designing various (machine learning) algorithms to solve core NLP problems, and creating user-friendly interfaces for other people/systems to use my tools.

I have most experience with Python, Java, AI in the context of NLP, linguistics, competitive programming.
My work so far spans within various areas of technical support and automation, as well as speech/text processing, sequence labeling, machine translation for low-resourced languages, information retrieval, and language modeling.
